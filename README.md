# Padlet Background Chooser

A beautiful, interactive single-page website that lets Padlet users choose meaningful optional backgrounds that represent their identity, passions, beliefs, pets, sports, professions, and more.

## ✨ Features

- **70+ curated options** across many categories:
  - Solid Colors
  - Pride & Rainbows
  - Religious & Spiritual Symbols
  - Music & Instruments
  - Sports & Activities
  - Lifestyle & Fashion
  - Playing Cards & Games
  - Professions & Careers
  - Pets & Animals
  - Space & Cosmos
  - Farming & Nature
  - Food & Drink
  - Fun Emojis & Misc
- Live preview showing exactly how the background will look in a Padlet post
- Custom photo upload with instant preview
- Search + category filters
- One-click "Copy choice" and "Use in Padlet" actions
- Fully responsive, modern dark UI
- 100% client-side (no backend needed)

## How to Add to Your Padlet

1. Open this site and let users browse & preview options.
2. Users select a background they love.
3. They click **"Use in Padlet"** or **"Copy choice"**.
4. In your Padlet, go to the **User Backgrounds**, **Introduce Yourself**, or a dedicated section.
5. Users post their choice (text + optional screenshot of the preview).

This creates a lovely personal touch and helps build community!

## Deploy / Self-Host

This is a single `index.html` file. Easy to host anywhere:

### GitHub Pages (recommended)
1. Go to your repo **Settings** → **Pages**
2. Under "Build and deployment" → Source: **Deploy from a branch**
3. Branch: `main`, Folder: `/ (root)`
4. Save
5. Wait ~1 minute → Your site will be live at `https://<username>.github.io/padlet-background-chooser/`
